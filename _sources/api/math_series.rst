Math Series
===========

Match a biosignal's peak ratios against classic mathematical sequences
(Fibonacci, Lucas, harmonics, Farey, …) to find which is most present, and
derive scales from the best match. Walkthrough: :doc:`../examples/math_series/math_series`.
